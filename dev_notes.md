Cool Terminal Stuff for Arch 🎉
Here are some bangers:

cmatrix — The Matrix rain effect. pacman -S cmatrix then just run cmatrix. Green code rain. Very hacker. 🟩
sl — You typo ls as sl and a steam locomotive drives across your terminal. Punishment for bad typing 🚂
cowsay — A cow says whatever you tell it. cowsay "moo". Pair with fortune | cowsay for random wisdom from a cow 🐄
lolcat — Pipes any text through a rainbow colorizer. ls | lolcat. Everything is better with rainbows 🌈
figlet / toilet — Giant ASCII text banners. figlet "LANTERN". toilet adds color effects
pipes.sh — Animated pipes screensaver in your terminal. pacman -S bash-pipes (AUR: pipes.sh)
cbonsai — Grows a random bonsai tree in your terminal. Zen af 🌳
asciiquarium — An aquarium in your terminal. Fish swim. It's beautiful 🐟
neofetch (or fastfetch) — The classic "flex your system specs" tool. Arch users are legally required to screenshot this
hollywood — Makes your terminal look like a Hollywood hacking scene. Multiple panes of fake busy stuff
tty-clock — Big beautiful clock in your terminal
htop / btop — System monitors that look gorgeous. btop is especially pretty
Combo move: fortune | cowsay | lolcat = rainbow cow dispensing wisdom 🐄🌈✨
